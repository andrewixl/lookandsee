Goals

Look & See Goals

Our goal is to equip people with the tools to become informed and critical navigators of the potentially volatile and insidious realm of online social media. Through a browser extension we aim to give users the option to know more about who they may be interacting with and use that to think critically about the content they are being presented with online, and through our website we aim to help educate parents and kids about the dangerous pathways to radicalization that exist online.

Why

Why did we Create Look & See

From petty trolls to malicious misinformers, you may come across online users who are not what they seem and who may not have your best interests in mind. It's never a bad idea to take a step back when you come across inflammatory posts and take a moment to consider who it is you’re talking to. Take a look and see more context around those you’re interacting with online
