Use in Popular Browsers

Look & See is availible on all chromium based browsers to keep you safe without changing your ways or interupting your day.

Sync Across Browsers

Look & See will sync across all your browsers when you sign in whether it be your home, work, or family comptuer.

Set your Own Privacy Level

You decide what gets blured and if you want to see it. We only blur and notify you why. We still allow you to uncover and see what was blured at anytime.

Protect your Friends and Family!

You can add friends and family to your Look & See account and set their privacy level or let them set their own.

  • Syncs across multiple computers and save time so you don't set up multiple accounts.
  • Set parental controls for kids and teens to protect them as well.
  • See data across those added to your account to see what is blured or unblured the most.
